张金明(Jinming Zhang)

张金明，出生于中国甘肃省金昌市永昌县。目前在西安交通大学电子与信息工程学院攻读博士学位。 他分别于2011年和2012年获得西安交通大学自动化专业学士学位与硕士学位。他的主要研究兴趣为：视觉目标跟踪， 图像匹配，特征提取，机器学习等。

Jinming Zhang is currently a Ph.D. candidate of State Key Laboratory for Manufacturing Systems Engineering, School of the Electronic and Information Engineering, Xi’an Jiaotong University, China. He received his Bachelor and Master degrees in Department of Automation from Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2011 and 2012, respectively. His research interests include visual tracking, image matching, feature extraction, and machine learning.

张金鹏(Jinpeng Zhang)

张金鹏，出生于中国甘肃省金昌市永昌县。目前在中国科学院自动化研究所攻读博士学位。 他分别于2011年和2014年获得华中科技大学自动化专业学士学位与硕士学位。他的主要研究兴趣为： 深度学习，模式识别，计算机视觉，类脑计算等。

Jinpeng Zhang is currently a Ph.D. candidate of Institute of Automation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA). He received his Bachelor and Master degrees in Department of Automation from Huazhong University of Science & Technology, China, in 2011 and 2014, respectively. His research interests include deep learning, pattern recognition, computer vision, brain-like computing.
